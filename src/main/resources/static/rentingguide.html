  • Monthly Rent

Offer Process

Submitting an Offer

Once youâ€™ve found have the home you like, you must submit your offer to rent the property from the landlord.

To submit your documents, you will typically require your personal documents, a rental application, and an offer to rent outlining the terms you wish to negotiate.

Terms

Your terms will include how much you are offering to rent the home for - your deposit amount - the length of the term - what services are included and your additional charges - the occupancy date and other relavent details.

After submission

Once your offer has been submitted, the landlord will have the choice of either accepting, rejecting, or countering your offer.

Once an offer has been accepted, you typically have 24 hours to provide your deposit.
